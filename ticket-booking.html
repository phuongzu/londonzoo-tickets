Terms & Conditions

1. Ticket Purchase

All tickets are non-refundable and non-transferable. Tickets are valid only for the date shown. We may be able to change your booking date if you contact us at least 48 hours before your visit date.

2. Entry Conditions

The zoo reserves the right to refuse entry. All visitors must comply with our code of conduct. Children under 16 must be accompanied by an adult at all times.

3. Photography

Photography is permitted for personal use only. Commercial photography requires prior permission. Drones are not permitted on site.

4. Safety

Follow all safety instructions from staff. Do not feed the animals or cross safety barriers. Some areas may be closed for animal welfare reasons.

5. Weather

The zoo is open in all weather conditions. Some animals may not be visible in extreme weather. Tickets are not refundable due to weather.

6. COVID-19

We follow all government guidelines. You may be required to wear face coverings in indoor areas. Please do not visit if you have COVID-19 symptoms.

7. Data Protection

We will use your personal data in accordance with our Privacy Policy. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time.

COVID-19 Safety Measures

Your Safety is Our Priority

We've implemented enhanced cleaning and safety measures to protect our visitors, staff, and animals.

Before You Visit

  • Book online in advance to guarantee entry
  • Do not visit if you or anyone in your household has COVID-19 symptoms
  • Download our app for contactless maps and information

During Your Visit

  • Face coverings are recommended in all indoor areas
  • Use hand sanitizer stations located throughout the zoo
  • Maintain social distancing where possible
  • Follow one-way systems where indicated

Enhanced Measures

  • Increased cleaning of high-touch areas
  • Protective screens at service points
  • Contactless payment preferred
  • Reduced capacity in indoor exhibits

Animal Welfare

Some animals may not be on display if our veterinary team determines it's in their best interest. We appreciate your understanding.
